Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation 100% used for beneficiaries?

Yes! 100% of your donation goes directly to the beneficiary. Our operational costs are covered separately by dedicated donors.

Will I get a receipt?

Absolutely. We provide official receipts for all donations within 24 hours of confirmation.

Can I donate for a specific case?

Yes! Visit our Active Cases page and mention the case ID when sending your donation confirmation.

How do I know my donation was used properly?

We send updates with photos and receipts showing how your donation was distributed. Check our Transparency Reports for monthly details.

Is Zakat distributed according to Islamic guidelines?

Yes. All Zakat donations are distributed only to Zakat-eligible recipients as per Islamic Shariah. We maintain separate records for Zakat and Sadaqah.
