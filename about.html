About Ismail Welfare Trust

Transforming Lives Through Verified Support & 100% Transparency

Our Mission

Ismail Welfare Trust (IWT) is a non-profit charitable organization dedicated to helping underprivileged individuals and families across Pakistan. We believe in creating a transparent and trustworthy platform where every rupee donated reaches those who truly need it.

Founded with the vision of eliminating poverty through verified support, we connect generous donors with beneficiaries who have been thoroughly vetted through home visits and document verification.

Our commitment is simple: 100% transparency, 100% impact. Every donation is tracked, every case is verified, and every donor receives regular updates on how their contribution made a difference.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

100% Transparency

Every donation is tracked and reported. Monthly financial reports published for complete accountability.

Verified Cases Only

We personally verify every case through home visits and document checks before publishing.

Direct Impact

100% of your donation goes to the beneficiary. No administrative deductions from charity funds.

Islamic Compliance

All operations follow Islamic principles. Zakat distributed only to eligible recipients.

Community Driven

We work with local communities to identify genuine cases and ensure proper distribution.

Regular Updates

Donors receive updates with photos and receipts showing exactly how donations were used.
